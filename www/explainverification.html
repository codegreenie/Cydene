Verification Process

Gas sellers/suppliers on Cydene Express are required to upload a document of identification. These includes:

Personal Identification/Proof of Residence
  • National ID Card
  • Driver's License
  • International Passport
  • National Voter's Card
  • Bank Account Statement
  • Utility Bill
  • C.A.C. document


Send an email containing your company name and registered(on Cydene) phone number to verify@cydene.com with your document as an attachment. Verification takes 24 hours.
