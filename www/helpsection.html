Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Cydene Express?

    Cydene Express is a mobile app, a division of Cydene petrochemicals which allows you to order for cooking gas to be delivered to your home.

  • How do i order gas?

    Ordering for cooking gas is very easy with Cydene Express. On the dashboard(After signup or Login), Select your cylinder size and quantity, pick a delivery address, a list of sellers will be presented to you, select your desired seller and pay.

  • How do i pay for gas?

    There are 2 ways to pay for cooking gas with Cydene express. You can pay with your "Cydene Wallet" or pay cash on delivery.

  • Estimated Time of Delivery (ETD)?

    Estimated Time of Delivery(ETD) is the estimated amount of time for cooking gas to be delivered to you. Factors to consider are distance between you and the seller and traffic conditions

  • Scheduled Bookings

    The is is a feature of Cydene thats allows you to order for gas on a later date. Once this date reaches, We assign a nearby seller to match your order.

  • Refunds?

    In the event of a refund, Cydene credits your wallet and can be used to pay for subsequent orders.

  • How do i get coupons?

    Coupons give discounts to buyers when applied. Coupon codes can be retrieved from the coupon section of the application
